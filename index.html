Committed to the Oceans

At Stackly, sustainability is not just a buzzword—it's the core of our operations. We utilize closed-loop Recirculating Aquaculture Systems (RAS) to minimize environmental impact.

  • 90% reduction in water usage compared to traditional farming.
  • Zero-discharge waste management systems.
  • 100% renewable energy utilized in core facilities.

Frequently Asked Questions

What species can your systems monitor?

Our vision AI and water quality sensors are optimized for a wide range of species including Salmon, Tilapia, Shrimp (Litopenaeus vannamei), and Barramundi. Custom models can be trained for other species upon request.

Do you offer international shipping and installation?

Yes, we ship our hardware globally and provide remote installation support. For large-scale enterprise deployments, we can dispatch our engineering team to your facility.

How accurate is the biomass estimation?

Our Gen-4 machine vision achieves a 98% accuracy rate in biomass estimation under optimal water clarity, and over 92% in highly turbid conditions.

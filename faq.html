Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our technology, sustainable practices, and aquaculture consulting services.

Our Recirculating Aquaculture Systems (RAS) are highly modular and can be customized for a wide variety of aquatic life, including Atlantic Salmon, Tilapia, Pacific White Shrimp, Mud Crabs, and various ornamental fish species.
Yes! We offer extensive aquaculture consultancy services. Our marine biologists and engineers can assess your existing open-pond or indoor facility and provide a roadmap to integrate IoT sensors, smart feeding, and AI health detection to drastically improve your yield.
We deploy submerged, high-definition optical cameras inside the net pens or tanks. These cameras feed data into our proprietary machine vision models that look for erratic swimming patterns, physical lesions, or parasites (like sea lice). If an anomaly is detected, an alert is sent directly to your Stackly Dashboard.
Absolutely. Our IoT water quality sensors are built with military-grade, anti-corrosive titanium housing and self-cleaning optical wipers, ensuring accurate readings for dissolved oxygen, pH, salinity, and ammonia even in harsh, bio-fouling saltwater environments.
The Stackly Automation Dashboard receives telemetry data from the IoT farm network every 5 seconds. This real-time streaming allows for immediate actuation of pumps, valves, and automated feeders.
In our indoor facilities, we utilize a strict zero-discharge policy. Solid waste and bio-sludge are mechanically captured, dried, and repurposed into high-grade organic fertilizers, which we distribute to local terrestrial agriculture operations.

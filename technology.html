Our Technology

Revolutionizing aquaculture through Artificial Intelligence, IoT integrations, and automated robotics to maximize efficiency and minimize environmental footprint.

IoT Monitoring

Continuous data collection from thousands of connected sensors across all farming units, providing real-time environmental context and instant anomaly alerts.

AI Fish Health Detection

Machine vision models analyze underwater camera feeds to detect early signs of disease, stress behaviors, and physical parasites before they spread.

Smart Feeding Systems

Automated dispensers paired with acoustic sensors that listen to feeding frenzy activity, cutting off feed exactly when the fish are full to prevent waste.

Water Quality Sensors

Military-grade optical and chemical sensors measuring DO, pH, salinity, and ammonia levels with high-precision accuracy 24 hours a day.

Drone Monitoring

Autonomous underwater ROVs and aerial drones that map net pens, inspect infrastructure, and monitor off-shore structural integrity without human divers.

Automation Dashboard

A centralized, cloud-based platform accessible from any device, giving farm managers complete remote control over pumps, valves, and feeding schedules.
